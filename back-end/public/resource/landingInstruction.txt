<p><span style="white-space:pre-wrap;"><span style="font-size: 48px;"><strong>How to upload your recipe</strong><span style="font-size: 48px;"><br></span></span><span style="font-size: 36px;">to Don’t forget your Recipe</span></span></p>
<p><br></p>
<p><span style="white-space: pre-wrap; font-size: 18px;">Have you ever created any dishes that you want to share with your friends? Or have you ever wanted a place to simply hold all your recipes? This can be easily achieved if you followed the instruction below. All recipes can be found in the personal kitchen, accessible via the top menu bar.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;"><br></span></p>
<p><span style="white-space: pre-wrap; font-size: 18px;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">Step 1:<br>Open the Personal Kitchen and click on the New Recipe at the left menu bar.<br></span><strong>Choose a&nbsp;recipe title and upload a picture of your dish.</strong><span style="font-size: 18px;"><br></span></span></p>
<p><span style="white-space: pre-wrap; font-size: 18px;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><br>Step 2:<br>Let’s continue with some&nbsp;general information about your recipe.
</span></span></p>
<p><span style="white-space: pre-wrap; font-size: 18px;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>- Serving size:</strong><span style="font-size: 18px;">&nbsp;In order for the community to estimate the amount of the dish, specify the servings.
</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="white-space: pre-wrap; font-size: 18px;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>- Prepare time:</strong><span style="font-size: 18px;">&nbsp;Let the community know the approximate preparation time this recipe is going to take. This preparation time will include from prepare for the ingredient to serving the dish on the table. </span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="white-space: pre-wrap; font-size: 18px;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>- Courses:</strong><span style="font-size: 18px;"> Please specify the course type of this recipe so that the community can easily locate their target. </span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="white-space: pre-wrap; font-size: 18px;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>- Other Categories:</strong><span style="font-size: 18px;"> By adding some more specific categories can make the community know more about this recipe. The categories could be for example soup, salad, holiday, vegetarian, religious, ketogenic diet…<br></span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="white-space: pre-wrap; font-size: 18px;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><br>Step 3:<br>Before cooking, you usually go shopping and prep your ingredients—right? This is exactly why we also start your recipe with the&nbsp;ingredients. Here, you need to include all the ingredients that will be used for your dish.
 You can easily type the ingredient into the given field. Then, enter the amount that’s needed.
 Some ingredients may need important characteristics. Let’s say you’re preparing the dough and it’s crucial that the butter is cold. For these kinds of ingredients, we’ve already set up a list of characteristics to choose from. 
In addition, you have the option to choose the unit that you are more familiar with. Once you’ve added an ingredient you can move on to the procedures.<br></span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="white-space: pre-wrap; font-size: 18px;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><br>Step 4:<br>Each recipe is divided into several steps that describe the cooking process in chronological order. Write a description for each step that tells what needs to be done.
 In addition, you can&nbsp;upload a photo or video&nbsp;to each step to help others visualize it.
</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="white-space: pre-wrap; font-size: 18px;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><br></span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="white-space: pre-wrap; font-size: 18px;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><span style="font-size: 18px;">Step 5:<br>In the description section, it is important to keep reminded of important information about this recipe, such as contains ingredients that may cause allergies, suitable community, suggestions for different flavors, and so on. </span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="white-space: pre-wrap; font-size: 18px;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><span style="font-size: 18px;"><br>Step 6:<br>By now you have entered all the information about your recipe. Read carefully through all the steps and check whether there are any spelling mistakes, or if you forgot to add an ingredient or utensil. By clicking&nbsp;“Save New Recipe”&nbsp;you can save your recipe to your personal kitchen space.</span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;"><br></span></p>
<p><span style="white-space: pre-wrap; font-size: 18px;">That was easy!<br>Your recipe <strong style="">is now live</strong>. You can see it in your personal kitchen space next to all the other recipes uploaded or liked by you. Your recipe can be open to the whole community, by simply making it public. Then it can be liked, commented on, and cooked! 
You can publish and share as many recipes as you want and we’re excited about what you and your friends come up with! Be creative, dig out the family cookbook, and share it with your friends. Have fun and don’t forget your recipe!</span><br></p>
